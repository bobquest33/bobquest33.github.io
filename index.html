My First Octopress Blog

Dec 24th, 2013

In my quest for better blogging engines and ways of hacking my way through to create a automated blog. I stumbled upon Octopress. The combination of ruby, jekyll, sinatra and the beauty of commandline makes Octopress a nice way to try at blogging for me.
