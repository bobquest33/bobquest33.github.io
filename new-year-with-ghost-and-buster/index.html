New Year with Ghost and Buster

03 January 2015

2014 had been a very uneventfull year when it comes to blogging. My tumbler blog basically is for the stuffs that I read over the internet and is synced with my Twitter. My Blogger account and Wordpress accouts have been lying idle for a while.

My Githib blog that was based on octopress had been also lying idle and had a old design. Hence I thought which can be the best way to restart my technical blogging as I am dedicating 2015 for blogging. The first contender was Ghost. Its simplicity and ease of use has made me love it and also it means I can host it anytime I want.

I am using the below links to setup Ghost using docker and buster. And using these to
publish my blog in github.

  • Docker & Ghost Installation

https://www.packtpub.com/books/content/simple-blog-deployment-using-ghost-and-docker

  • Static site generation with Ghost & Buster https://ghost.org/forum/services/1667-static-site-generator-with-easy-upload-to-github-pages/

If you are seeing this then its written out of Docker-Ghost and extracted and deployed via Buster.
